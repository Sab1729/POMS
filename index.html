About the Society

The People of Mathematics Society, or POM Society, was established on 27th February 2022. The main objective of the Society is to Learn & Teach Mathematics and its various forms.

The Society's Oath

Every person is born equally and must be treated equally.

Knowledge is free and must be given to everyone freely.

We promise to make Knowledge free and equal to all.

We will work hard in this Modern era of Competition and invoke the spirit of Companionship.

Therefore, we will not stay idle or inactive. Nor shall we keep any mathematical secrets to ourselves.

We strive to work for a better world for all.

How do I become a Member

i) Direct Membership: The person interested in joining the Society shall write the Mathematical Society Examination (MSE), conducted a few times every year.

ii) Indirect Membership: Membership is granted when the person interested in joining the Society gets a specific number of votes, from the Society Members and the Council Members, to join the Society. In order to apply for such membership, you must write a mail to the current President of the Society.
